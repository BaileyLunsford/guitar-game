Frequently Asked Questions

The microphone isn't detecting my notes

Make sure you've granted microphone permission to the app. Go to iOS Settings → Guitar Audition Game → Microphone and make sure it's enabled. Play close to your device and in a quiet environment for best results.

The tuner isn't responding

Tap "Enable Microphone" on the tuner screen and allow access when prompted. The tuner works best in a quiet room.

The app crashed or froze

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, please email us with your device model and iOS version and we'll look into it right away.

How do I replay the onboarding tour?

Tap the ⚙️ Settings button on the home screen and select "Replay Tour."
